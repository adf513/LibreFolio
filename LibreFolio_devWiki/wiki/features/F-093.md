# F-093 — Coupon Policy Field

## What it does
A `coupon_policy` enum field on `FAInterestRatePeriod` with values: `FULL_RESET` (full coupon on first period after change), `CUSTOM_RATE` (override rate for transition period), `PARTIAL` (pro-rata calculation). This handles edge cases in coupon calculations when interest rate periods change mid-coupon-cycle.
