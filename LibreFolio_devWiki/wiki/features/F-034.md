# F-034 — Scheduled Investment Provider (Synthetic Yield)

## What it does
A special asset provider for assets with a predefined payment schedule — loans, structured products, crowdfunding investments (e.g. Recrowd). Instead of fetching prices from a market, it computes the asset value synthetically from a schedule of interest rates and the investment timeline.

## Layer breakdown
**Backend**: `backend/app/services/asset_source_providers/scheduled_investment.py`
**Frontend**: `frontend/src/lib/components/assets/ScheduledInvestmentEditor.svelte` (1412 lines — most complex component)

## Dependencies

- [[F-059]] Provider Registry Pattern

## Enables

- [[F-030]] Price History — populated with computed values

## Implementation notes
- Asset type: `SCHEDULED_YIELD`
- `params_schema` is the most complex of all providers: defines interest schedule (date ranges + rates), face value, late interest
- Value formula: `unit_value = face_value + accrued_interest(date)` → `market_value = unit_value × quantity`
- Interest accrues continuously — daily granularity
- `ScheduledInvestmentEditor` component allows defining multi-tier schedules visually
- The provider acts as both data source (generates prices) and asset type definition
